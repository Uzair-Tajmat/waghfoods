Classic

Classic Veg Hakka Noodles

🕑 15 mins 🍽 Serves 2 🔥 Easy

The traditional way to enjoy Kitchen Express noodles - stir-fried with fresh vegetables and our signature seasoning.

Quick & Easy Family Favorite Vegetarian

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack Kitchen Express Veg Hakka Noodles (150g)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (cabbage, carrot, capsicum, spring onion)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Included seasoning packet

Instructions:

  1. Boil noodles in salted water for 3-4 minutes until soft. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or large pan over high heat.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until slightly tender but still crisp.
  4. Add the boiled noodles and toss well with vegetables.
  5. Add soy sauce, seasoning packet, salt, and pepper. Stir-fry for another 2 minutes.
  6. Garnish with spring onion greens and serve hot!
Spicy

Spicy Schezwan Noodles

🕑 20 mins 🍽 Serves 2 🔥 Medium

Add a fiery kick to your noodles with schezwan sauce - perfect for those who love spice!

Spicy Indo-Chinese Party Favorite

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack Kitchen Express Veg Hakka Noodles (150g)
  • 2 tbsp schezwan sauce
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • Red chili flakes to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil noodles according to package instructions. Drain and toss with a little oil to prevent sticking.
  2. Heat oil in a wok. Add minced garlic and saute until fragrant.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry on high heat for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add schezwan sauce and mix well with vegetables.
  5. Add noodles, soy sauce, and vinegar. Toss everything together.
  6. Sprinkle chili flakes, adjust seasoning, and serve hot!
Snack

Crispy Noodle Bhel

🕑 10 mins 🍽 Serves 4 🔥 Easy

A fun fusion snack that combines crispy fried noodles with tangy chutneys - perfect for parties!

Crispy Party Snack No-Cook

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack Kitchen Express Veg Hakka Noodles (deep fried and crushed)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup boiled potato, cubed
  • 2 tbsp green chutney
  • 2 tbsp tamarind chutney
  • Sev and coriander for garnish
  • Chaat masala to taste

Instructions:

  1. Deep fry the noodles in hot oil until crispy and golden. Drain and let cool. Crush lightly.
  2. In a large bowl, combine crispy noodles, onion, tomato, and potato.
  3. Add green chutney, tamarind chutney, and chaat masala.
  4. Toss everything together gently.
  5. Top with sev and fresh coriander.
  6. Serve immediately while the noodles are still crispy!
Appetizer

Noodle Spring Rolls

🕑 30 mins 🍽 Makes 12 🔥 Medium

Crispy on the outside, flavorful noodle filling inside - these spring rolls are a crowd pleaser!

Appetizer Party Perfect Crispy

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack Kitchen Express Veg Hakka Noodles (150g)
  • 12 spring roll sheets
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup grated carrot
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • Flour paste for sealing
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. Boil noodles until just done. Drain and chop into smaller pieces.
  2. Saute cabbage and carrot in sesame oil. Add noodles and soy sauce. Cool the mixture.
  3. Place 2 tbsp filling on each spring roll sheet. Fold the sides and roll tightly.
  4. Seal edges with flour paste.
  5. Deep fry in hot oil until golden and crispy.
  6. Serve hot with sweet chili sauce or schezwan dip!
Indo-Chinese

Veg Manchurian Noodles

🕑 35 mins 🍽 Serves 3 🔥 Medium

The best of both worlds - crispy hakka noodles topped with saucy veg manchurian balls!

Restaurant Style Indo-Chinese Hearty Meal

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack Kitchen Express Veg Hakka Noodles
  • For Manchurian: 1 cup mixed veggies (finely chopped), 3 tbsp corn flour, salt
  • For Gravy: 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp chili sauce, 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp garlic-ginger paste
  • 2 tbsp corn flour slurry
  • Spring onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Mix chopped veggies with corn flour and salt. Form small balls and deep fry until golden.
  2. Prepare noodles according to classic recipe and keep warm.
  3. For gravy: Saute garlic-ginger, add sauces, water, and corn flour slurry. Cook until thick.
  4. Add fried manchurian balls to the gravy.
  5. Serve hot noodles topped with manchurian gravy and spring onions!

Cooking Tips

Get the perfect noodles every time

🍳

Don't Overcook

Boil noodles for just 3-4 minutes to keep them firm and chewy

💧

Rinse After Boiling

Quick cold water rinse stops cooking and removes excess starch

🔥

High Heat Stir-Fry

Use high heat for that authentic wok-tossed flavor

🥦

Oil Your Noodles

Toss drained noodles in a little oil to prevent sticking
