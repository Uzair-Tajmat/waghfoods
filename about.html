Our Story

From Kitchen to Your Heart

Kitchen Express, a proud brand of Wagh's Food Products Co., was born from a simple vision - to bring authentic, high-quality noodles to every Indian household. Based in Bhiwandi, Maharashtra, we started our journey with a commitment to quality that remains unchanged today.

What began as a small family venture has grown into a trusted name in the food industry. Our signature Veg Hakka Noodles are crafted using traditional recipes combined with modern food safety standards, ensuring every pack delivers the perfect crispy, crunchy experience.

Today, Kitchen Express products are enjoyed by families across India, and we continue to innovate while staying true to our roots - pure, healthy, and absolutely delicious!

Our mission is to deliver premium quality noodles that bring families together. We believe that good food creates lasting memories, and we're committed to being a part of those special moments.

Meet Wagh's Food Products

Wagh's Food Products Co. is the proud parent company behind Kitchen Express. Our tiger mascot represents our fierce commitment to quality and the bold, authentic flavors we bring to your table.
